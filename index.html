Control, discipline, flexibility, balance: learning a martial art teaches you all of these things in body, mind and soul.

With the Bath branch of Wutan, the UK's oldest traditional school of Chinese martial arts, you can learn different arts such as Tai Chi and Kung Fu to suit your particular needs and focus.

Beginners are always welcome in any class, although there are some classes specifically tailored to beginners. We also have some classes specifically for children.
